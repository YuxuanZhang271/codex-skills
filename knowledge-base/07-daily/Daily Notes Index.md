# Daily Notes

Use daily notes for fast capture, then promote durable material into permanent notes.

## Daily Workflow

- Capture tasks, readings, ideas, and meeting fragments.
- Link to relevant project, paper, concept, or writing notes.
- At the end of the day or week, move stable context into the right folder.
