# Medical Robotics

Medical robotics applies robotic perception, planning, and control to diagnosis, treatment, surgery, rehabilitation, and image-guided intervention.

## Questions To Track

- What clinical task is being improved?
- What sensing and imaging modalities are available?
- What level of autonomy is appropriate?
- What safety, latency, and interpretability constraints dominate?
